Learn to play!

Our talented roster of 20+ instructors offer a wide variety of instrument lessons for all ages. Whether you’re just starting out or looking to expand your abilities, our teachers are passionate about working with you to meet your musical goals!

Tony Nice

Bluegrass,  Blues,  Folk,  Jazz Guitar

Tony Nice is a renowned music instructor and acoustic guitarist celebrated for his soulful blend of bluegrass, folk, and jazz. Growing up in the Appalachian hills, Tony developed a unique flatpicking style that pays homage to traditional acoustic music while showcasing his innovative flair. For over 20 years, Tony has been inspiring students of all levels, teaching them not just technique but the heart of storytelling through music. When he's not teaching, Tony performs at festivals and workshops, sharing his passion for the timeless art of acoustic guitar.
